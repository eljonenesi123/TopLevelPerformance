Meet the coach

Jeart Kumi

I'm a certified personal trainer, corrective exercise specialist and nutritionist based in Tirana, Albania with experience intraining clients at Square Wellness Center and coaching athletes online across the world.

My background started in football. I spent 2 years coaching youth players from U5 all the way through to U15, developing training programs built around movement quality, athleticism, and long-term development. That foundation shaped how I think about training: technique first, intensity second, and progression always.

I moved into personal training because I wanted to work with people one-on-one and build programs that actually fit around their life, not generic plans copied from the internet. Every client I work with gets a program designed from scratch, tracked weekly, and adjusted based on real results.

Outside the gym, I hold three ISSA certifications: Certified Personal Trainer, Corrective Exercise Specialist, and Nutritionist, so whether you want to get stronger, lose weight, fix movement patterns, or clean up your diet, I can coach all of it in one place.

How I coach

Training philosophy

Technique first

Every program starts with movement quality. Good form under load is what separates progress from injury — I correct it live, on every rep.

Progressive overload

Effort without direction is just hard work. Every plan tracks your numbers week to week so the progress is measurable, not guesswork.

Built around you

No templates. Your schedule, your equipment, your goals. The program fits your life — not the other way around.
